# Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 1 1/2 cups of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 3/4 cups of flour
- 2 tsp cream of tartar
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp table salt

# Instructions

- Mix butter and sugar until creamy with a beater (~5 mins)
- add in eggs then mix until blended
- To the butter mixture, now sift in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt
- Refrigerate dough for half an hours
- Make cute lil balls and roll in a cinnamon sugar mixture
- Bake @ 350 for 15 mins
