# Ingredients

- 285 grams (hot) water
- 30 grams olive oil
- 9 grams salt
- 30 grams sugar
- 390 grams white flour
- 7 grams instant yeast (make sure salt and yeast dont touch lol)

# Instructions

- 🥣 add ingredients from wet to dry ending with yeast on top in a little welt above the flour
- 🧑‍🍳 In a breadmaker, select menu item 1, 1.5 lb loaf, and dark crust
