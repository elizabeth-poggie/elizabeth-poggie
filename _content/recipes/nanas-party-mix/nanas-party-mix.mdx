# Ingredients

- 6 Tbsp butter
- 4 tsp worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp seasoned salt (or 3/8 tsp garlic powder && 3/8 tsp salt)
- 6 cups chex (wheat && corn)
- 3/4 cups salted nutes

# Ingredients

- set oven to 250 F
- slowly melt butter in a shallow pan- still all ingrediants, coating chex && nuts
- cook in oven for 45 mins, stirring every 15 minutes
