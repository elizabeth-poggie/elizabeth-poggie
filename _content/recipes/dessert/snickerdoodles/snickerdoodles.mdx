# Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 1 1/2 cups of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 3/4 cups of flour
- 2 tsp cream of tartar
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp table salt

# Instructions

- 🧈 Mix Butter and Sugar: Beat the butter and sugar together until creamy (about 5 minutes).
- 🥚 Add Eggs: Mix in the eggs until well blended.
- 🥣 Combine Dry Ingredients: Sift the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt into the butter mixture and mix until combined.
- ⏰ Chill Dough: Refrigerate the dough for 30 minutes.
- 🧥 Shape and Coat: Make cute lil balls and roll them in a cinnamon sugar mixture.
- 🧑‍🍳 Bake @ 350°F for 15 minutes.
